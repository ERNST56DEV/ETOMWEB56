Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Eng. Etom Richard, Etom Engineering Services Limited began as a small civil engineering firm in Gulu, Uganda. With just five employees, we took on local construction projects, quickly earning a reputation for quality and reliability.

Over the past 7 years, we've grown into a multidisciplinary engineering firm with over 20 employees, serving clients across Uganda. Our expansion has been guided by our commitment to technical excellence, innovation, and sustainable development.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, sustainable engineering solutions that transform communities in Northern Uganda while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the leading engineering services provider in Northern Uganda, recognized for technical excellence, community impact, and contribution to regional development.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Quality, Safety, Sustainability, and Community Engagement guide every project we undertake and every decision we make.
