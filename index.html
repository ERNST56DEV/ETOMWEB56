About Etom Engineering Services Limited Technical Services Limited

Founded in 2015, Etom Engineering Services Limited Technical Services Limited has grown to become a leading engineering and construction firm in Uganda. Our team of certified professionals is committed to delivering innovative solutions that meet international standards while addressing local challenges.

With headquarters in Gulu City, we have successfully completed over 50 projects across the region, earning a reputation for reliability, quality, and timely delivery.
